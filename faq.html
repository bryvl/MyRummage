The recipe given has ingredients that I did not list

Yes, this does happen.
The cocktail receipe integration that we use compiles many different recipes from the ingredients that you list.
Sometimes this results in an ingredient list that contains more than what you have.
In this case we suggest saving the recipe to try another time, try an abbrevated version of what you do have,
or ask your neighbor if they have the missing ingredient.
We'd never tell you to go to the store, though. (yuck)

Is there a max ingredients for the search?

Yes, you can search up to 5 individual ingredients.
However, you can add the search pantry option which you can input as many items as you desire.

Do I have to create a pantry list?

Nope!
